# Demo Company HR Policy Handbook (India)

**Company:** Demo Tech India Pvt. Ltd.  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective:** 17 May 2026  

This is a **single-file demo policy** for RAG testing. It contains leave numbers, notice periods, WFH, reimbursement, and FAQs so chat queries can retrieve factual answers.

---

## 1. Annual leave entitlements (days per year)

| Leave type | Days per calendar year | Notes |
|------------|------------------------|--------|
| Casual leave | 12 | For short personal needs |
| Sick leave | 10 | Medical / illness |
| Earned leave / privilege leave | 15 | Accrues monthly; can carry forward as per policy |
| Maternity leave | 180 calendar days | Approx. 26 weeks; medical certificate required |
| Paternity leave | 10 consecutive days | Within 6 months of childbirth |

**Rules**

- All leave requests must be submitted in the **HR self-service portal** before the leave start date where possible.
- **Emergency sick leave**: inform your manager within **2 hours** of shift start.
- Leave taken without approval may be treated as **leave without pay (LWP)**.

---

## 2. Sick leave — quick facts

- Full-time employees in **India** get **10 paid sick leave days per year** as per the table in section 1.
- For sick leave longer than **2 consecutive working days**, HR may ask for a **medical certificate**.
- Unused sick leave **does not** cash out at resignation unless local law requires it (verify with HR).

---

## 3. Notice period

| Employee category | Notice period |
|-------------------|---------------|
| Intern | 15 days |
| Full-time employee | 30 days |
| Senior manager | 60 days |

**Full and final settlement** is typically completed within **45 days** of last working day after clearance.

---

## 4. Work from home (WFH)

- WFH is allowed **with manager approval**.
- You must be **available online** during core hours (**10:00–16:00 IST**) and attend scheduled meetings.
- **Company laptop and VPN** must be used for official work; do not store confidential data on personal devices.

---

## 5. Travel and reimbursement

Eligible claims:

- Official travel (train/flight as per grade)
- Client visits and local conveyance
- Hotel within approved limits

**Process:** Submit claims with **original bills** and manager approval in the portal within **30 days** of expense.  
**Approval flow:** Employee → Manager → Finance.

---

## 6. Payroll

- Salary is credited on the **last working day** of each month.
- Payslips are available in the **employee portal**.

---

## 7. Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

**Q1: How many sick leaves are allowed per year?**  
**A1:** Full-time employees are entitled to **10 sick leave days per calendar year** in India.

**Q2: How many casual leave days per year?**  
**A2:** **12 casual leave days** per calendar year.

**Q3: What is the notice period for a full-time employee?**  
**A3:** **30 days** for full-time employees (see section 3).

**Q4: How many earned leave days per year?**  
**A4:** **15 earned leave days** per calendar year.

**Q5: Can I work from home?**  
**A5:** Yes, **with your manager’s approval**, subject to WFH rules in section 4.

**Q6: How do I claim reimbursement?**  
**A6:** Use the HR portal with **bills and manager approval** within 30 days; see section 5.
